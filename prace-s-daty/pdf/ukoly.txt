Data are facts obtained from reality, e.g. measured physical quantities (temperature, pressure, weight), historical data, photographs, sound or film recordings, etc. 
The term information is used to refer to data that has already been evaluated and arranged into a form that has a certain meaning. Thanks to information, we create
